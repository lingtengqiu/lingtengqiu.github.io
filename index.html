I am a PhD student (from Sep. 2020) of School of Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hongkong, Shenzhen and under the supervision of Prof. Xiaoguang Han. Prior to CUHK-Shenzhen, I obtained M.S. from Department of Automation, Harbin Institue of Technology and B.S. from Fuzhou University(Rank: 1/113).

My research interests are in computer vision and machine learning. Specifically, learning based methods for 3D vision, human pose estimation, neural rendring, etc.
